Decades of curiosity

The Community Science Museum was founded in 1958 with the goal to excite ones curiosity with the wonders of science.

The museum contains exhibits from the fields of archeology, astronomy, chemistry, phisics, paleontology, modern technology and more!

Accessability

Our museum is highly accesible to individuals who use wheelchairs and people with visual and hearing impairments.

Please address our staff if you need assitive equipment and we will try our best to accommodate.
